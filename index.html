What is Calibration?

Calibration is the process of configuring an instrument to provide accurate measurements by comparing it with a known standard. This ensures instruments are working within specified tolerance and regulatory requirements.

Why is Calibration Required?

  • Ensures safety in process-critical industries
  • Maintains accuracy and consistency of measurement
  • Complies with ISO, pharma, and energy audit standards
  • Prevents costly errors or downtime due to drift
