Calibration of Instruments & Control Panels

Inaccuracy in measurement can have a serious impact on an organization ranging from production losses to potential safety hazards. To reduce such risks in both regulated and non-regulated environments, Vedora Engineering offers a wide range of calibration and compliance services.

Whether your concern is cost, quality, or time, the Vedora Engineering team is ready to support you.

Our professionals can design a customized calibration and service plan for your business that helps in testing and operations, controls validation costs, and reduces the difficulties of achieving and maintaining compliance.

Why Choose Vedora Engineering?

  • Precision calibration with full traceability, ensuring instruments meet OEM specifications and compliance requirements.
  • Our site execution team brings extensive experience across Oil & Gas, Pharmaceutical, and Chemical industries.
  • Field/Lab calibration services with traceable equipment.

Instruments We Calibrate

  • Analog / Digital Pressure Gauges
  • Vacuum Pressure Gauges
  • Pressure Transmitters
  • DPT, Level & Flow Transmitters
  • Temperature Gauges
  • Temperature Transmitters (RTD / Thermocouple)

Loop Checking & Troubleshooting Services

Faulty loops or unchecked connections can lead to signal errors, process interruptions, and even plant shutdowns. Ensuring smooth signal flow from field instruments to the control system is essential for safe and reliable operations.

Vedora Engineering provides comprehensive loop checking and troubleshooting support designed to verify signal integrity and system functionality. From pre-commissioning to live operations, our team ensures every loop is tested, confirmed, and documented.

What Makes Vedora Different?

  • Complete verification of instrument signals from field to control room.
  • Detection of wiring mistakes, polarity issues, grounding problems, and signal drop.
  • On-site troubleshooting during commissioning and routine operations.
  • Smooth integration with shutdown activities or project execution.
  • Clear documentation and updated loop records for client validation.

Loops & Systems We Handle

  • Pressure, Temperature, Flow, and Level transmitters.
  • Control valves, solenoids, and limit switches.
  • Safety interlocks and emergency shutdown systems.
  • Junction box to control panel connections.
  • PLC/DCS input-output mapping.

Instrumentation Installation Services

Instrumentation installation is not just about placing equipment in the field—it’s about precision, reliability, and long-term performance. Even a small error in installation can lead to inaccurate signals, safety issues, or costly downtime. That’s why every step of our installation process is carried out with technical accuracy and industrial best practices.

At Vedora Engineering, we deliver complete instrumentation installation solutions for projects, maintenance, and shutdowns. From mounting instruments to laying cables, our team ensures your system is set up correctly, tested thoroughly, and ready for seamless integration with your plant operations.

Why Choose Vedora Engineering for Installation?

Our services combine technical expertise, robust field execution, and compliance with standards. We cover every aspect of the installation cycle, including:

  • Instrument & Equipment Installation

    Professional installation of transmitters, gauges, and field instruments with proper sealing and alignment to ensure accuracy and reliability.

  • Mounting Stand Fabrication & Installation

    Customised stanchions and supports, fabricated and installed with precise orientation, correct slope for impulse lines, and long-term durability.

  • Cable Tray Installation with Supporting Arrangement

    Supply and installation of ladder, perforated, and GI/FRP trays, complete with strong supports, covers, and alignment for safe routing.

  • Cable Laying & Termination

    Primary cabling (JB to Control Room) and secondary cabling (Instrument to JB) executed with proper glanding, ferruling, tagging, and earthing to ensure noise-free, safe, and reliable connections.

By choosing Vedora, you’re assured of well-executed installations that minimize downtime, reduce maintenance headaches, and deliver long-term performance across your critical processes.

Design & Fabrication of Instrumentation Panels

Control panels are the nerve center of any plant—they connect the field instruments, operators, and automation systems into one reliable framework. A poorly designed or fabricated panel can result in wiring errors, space limitations, overheating, and operational failures.

At Vedora Engineering, we provide end-to-end panel design and fabrication services, ensuring your control systems are built with precision, safety, and long-term reliability. From engineering drawings to shop fabrication and site installation, our team delivers panels that are ready for integration and performance.

Why Choose Vedora Engineering for Panels?

We combine engineering expertise, quality fabrication, and strict compliance with standards to deliver panels that meet your project and process needs. Our offerings include:

  • Panel Design & Engineering

    Development of General Arrangement (GA) drawings, wiring diagrams, terminal layouts, and load calculations.

    I/O mapping and signal allocation for smooth integration with PLC/DCS systems.

  • Control Panel Fabrication

    Fabrication of PLC, DCS, RTU, and relay panels with precise wiring and tested layouts.

    Assembly of MCC and marshalling panels as per OEM and project specifications.

  • Component Sizing & Integration

    Proper selection and sizing of breakers, relays, power supplies, and wiring accessories.

    Compliance with IEC standards for safety, heat dissipation, and IP protection.

  • Testing & Documentation

    Pre-dispatch inspections, FAT (Factory Acceptance Test), and quality checks before shipment.

    Detailed test reports, wiring schedules, and as-built documentation for client records.

By partnering with Vedora, you receive panels that are not only robust and reliable, but also designed for easy maintenance, expansion, and long service life.
